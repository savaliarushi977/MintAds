# MintAds — Angle & Hook Selection Playbook (v2)

Stage-0 dropdowns + the selection logic + the psychology spine. Format = UGC video; sub-format is auto-derived from the angle.

---

## What Changed in v2

- New 8 personas
- Angles & hooks tagged with STEPPS lever + core psychology
- Added A10 (Social Currency) + 'Things I Wish I Knew' hook
- New Angle → UGC sub-format map (auto-pick, no new form field) with AI-generation feasibility for the Higgsfield/ElevenLabs pipeline

---

## How MintAds Uses It

1. User picks Persona + Journey + Brand → use the maps to surface the RECOMMENDED angles (don't weight all equally)
2. User picks an Angle → Angle × Hook map surfaces the recommended hooks; Angle → Sub-format map sets the UGC sub-format automatically
3. Journey sets the TONE the script engine applies (pre-trip = dreamy/aspirational; in-trip = urgent/actionable)
4. Brand sets the CTA + how forward the Headout brand is
5. Result: every combo is a coherent, share-worthy ad — never random

---

## The Psychology Spine (STEPPS + Tourism CB)

- **STEPPS (Berger)**: Social currency, Triggers, Emotion, Public, Practical value, Stories — all 6 covered across the 9 angles
- **Plus**: loss aversion/regret, social proof & authority (Cialdini), peak-end memory, pre-trip anticipation, identity signaling, high-risk-purchase de-risking
- **Note**: only travel-OTA-relevant formats/levers are included — nothing forced where it doesn't fit selling experiences

## Legend

- **●** = recommended (surface first)
- **○** = works (secondary)
- **blank** = avoid
- **Feasibility**: EASY / MED / HARD for the synthetic-creator pipeline

## Journey Guide

| Journey | Tone the script engine applies | Recommended angles | Recommended hooks | CTA energy |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-Trip | Aspirational, dreamy, 'add it to your list'. Sell the dream first (Emotion / anticipation). | A1, A2, A8, A7, A10 (A4, A5 work) | relatable_pov, curiosity, fomo, listicle, wish_i_knew | Inspire → 'Save it / Book ahead' |
| In-Trip | Urgent, actionable, now/tonight. Solve a problem in the moment (Triggers / scarcity). | A3, A14, A4 (A5, A7 work) | problem, outcome, fomo (mistake, social_proof work) | Act now → 'Book tonight / Skip the line now' |

## Brand Guide

| Brand mode | How the brand shows up | CTA | Angle lean |
|---|---|---|---|
| Headout | Brand front & centre; platform sells. Lean on Headout USPs (skip-line, free cancellation, trust at scale). | 'Book on Headout' | A3, A4, A5, A14 (any experience angle works with HO CTA) |
| Non-Headout | Experience-led; brand stays in the background. Pure experience emotion & discovery. | Generic: 'Book now / Get tickets' | A1, A8, A2, A7, A10 |
